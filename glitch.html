Glitch

Data Bending

Data bending is a multistep process of manipulating images to appear "glitched." The term "databending" comes from the concept of manipulating
media files of a certain format in software designed to edit files of another format. This glitch art was made by converting a JPG image file of a moon into a
raw file, importing it into Audacity, applying changes like echo+reverb, exporting as a raw file, and finally converting the edited raw file back into a JPG file.
