New Experiences

Perched atop the highest shelf in my living room, stood my great grandfather's dusty old camera bag. I've passed by this camera bag countless times, yet on one fateful evening, curiosity got the best of me. I took the bag down and carefully unzipped the top to take a peek inside. Inside the bag, lived an Argus C3, a light meter, and a handful of interchangeable lenses. I spent quite a bit of time fiddling with the lenses, pressing random buttons, and pulling different levers. I even took the camera to a local camera shop and asked them if it still worked. Unfortunately, it didn't. Nevertheless, I was determined to step into the shoes of my great grandfather, striving to capture magical moments on film. Little did I know, I was finding a new passion.
