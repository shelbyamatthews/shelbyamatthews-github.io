Llama Jump

Sprites created with Piskel. Developed with Unity & C#.

Llama Jump requires the player to navigate the platforms to retrieve a runaway llama. The player must use WASD or the arrow keys to move and use the space bar to jump.
Known problems: There isn't a solid "game won" condition. The player technically wins when they reach the llama, but there's no fanfare for now.
