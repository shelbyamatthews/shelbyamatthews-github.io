Light leaks are correct! Light leaks on C41 (color film) will either appear as orange, yellow, or white. On black and white film, they will appear as white. It's very popular to put light leak filters over digital photos. However, back in the day, this was incredibly frustrating and devastating. Many photos were destroyed and other copies weren't available.
