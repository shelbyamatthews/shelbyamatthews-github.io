p5.js Selfie

JavaScript

This graphic was written in JavaScript through p5.JS, an IDE geared for hosting a multitude of coding languages.
Knowledge and familiarity of the X & Y axes are essential when writing graphics in JavaScript.
This image predominantly consists of ellipses, arcs, and rectangles, all strategically placed on the correct axes.
