Shelby Matthews is a 19-year old Digital Media Art major from the East Bay Area studying at San Jose State
University. Eager to contribute to campus life, Shelby serves as an Adobe Student Representative,
where she collaborates with other Adobe Students to advertise the brilliance of the Creative Cloud.
Despite her underclassman status, Shelby stands out by enrolling in upper-division standing classes,
demonstrating her expertise and commitment in her field.

She has completed Art 74, which introduced her to front-end development, rapid prototyping, and
databending. Currently enrolled in Art/CS 108, she is excited to collaborate with her peers to design and
develop games using C# and Unity.

She dedicates her free time to photography (film & digital), augmented reality projects, and coding projects.
Matthews enjoys front-end development and aims to acquire proficiency in programming languages,
enhancing her toolkit as she pursues a minor in Computer Science.
